# Photos Directory

Upload your photos here! The photos modal will automatically display any images you add to this folder.

Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP
Recommended size: Under 10MB per image

## Instructions:
1. Add your photos to this `/public/photos/` directory
2. Click the "Photography" button in the navigation
3. Your photos will be displayed in a beautiful gallery
4. You can also upload photos directly through the modal interface

Enjoy showcasing your photography! 📸
